Introduction:

Hello and welcome to SuperCast! Here, you can create your dream cast for your dream movie. By assigning actors/actresses to specific characters, you can build the ideal cast for a movie named by you. build your SuperCast and share with your friends and families. Maybe you can inspire the next great movie!

Let's Get Started

Instructions:

Enter a name for your dream team or original movie into the input box. Then, select a character you wish to star in the movie and the actor or actress you would like to play that character. You can add additional rows to include other characters. Once you have completed your list of characters and actors, click "Submit Cast"
